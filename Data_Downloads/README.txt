Data and relevant info included in folders

"CENSUS_2016_CountyBoundaries" includes shapefiles for polygon analysis, if desired

"GHCN_ObservedData" includes NOAA observed historic data for certain Appalachian weather stations. Additional weather station for US locations can be found at:
https://www.ncei.noaa.gov/products/land-based-station/global-historical-climatology-network-daily

"NASA_NEX-GDDP_CMIP6_Models" includes download scripts and instructions for the NEX-GDDP CMIP6 Climate Models
